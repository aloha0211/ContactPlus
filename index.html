Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data might be collected from users of Contacts – from now on referred to as the app – how they are stored, processed and used by the app.

Your Provided Data

As a Contacts user, one may optionally allow the app to read your contacts and make phone calls. Once provided, your contacts will be used by the app for the sole purpose of making phone calls when you explicitly opt for that. This data is not retained or maintained outside the device and it remains there until you explicitly remove it via device’s settings, or when you uninstall the app.

Required Permissions

The application asks for the permissions for reading contacts, writing contacts, and making a phone call. The permission allows app to list down your contacts in the app and make phone calls using the app. The app does not attempt to manipulate any data in your device.

Collection of anonymous data for analytics

While you are interacting with the app, anonymous information regarding your device might be collected for the purpose of crash reporting and usage monitoring related to the app. The information collected helps identify app defects and popular features, that would serve as guidelines for the app’s future improvements. Only that Analytics data which Android platform allows without any extra permission is collected from the app. This data is not shared with any third party service except the ones that the app relies on for data collection.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
